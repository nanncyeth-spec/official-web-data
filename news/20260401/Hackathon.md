## [Announcement] HashKey Chain Horizon Hackathon Officially Launched 

When the intelligent decision-making capabilities of artificial intelligence meet the open and inclusive nature of decentralized finance, a technological wave disrupting the boundaries of financial innovation has arrived. Against this backdrop, HashKey Chain, Ethereum Application Guild (EAG), AWS Hong Kong, DoraHacks, and SeeGrowth jointly announce the official launch of the Horizon Hackathon. In Hong Kong, a city at the forefront of technology and finance, we invite developers, entrepreneurs, university teams, and industry practitioners worldwide to build across four tracks — DeFi, PayFi, AI, and ZKID — and shape the next generation of on-chain financial infrastructure.

- Registration Deadline: April 15, 2026 Participation

This hackathon primarily adopts an online format to lower participation barriers for global developers, while also providing continuous opportunities for technical exchange and ecosystem connection through online AMAs and offline events in Hong Kong.
<br></br>
HashKey Chain is committed to building on-chain financial infrastructure that can be adopted by real-world financial systems. The Horizon Hackathon is structured around four tracks grounded in real business scenarios, with systematic post-event support for winning and high-potential projects, including direct involvement from HashKey Group's investment, marketing, and compliance teams, alongside ecosystem partners spanning exchanges, security audit firms, and VCs, to help teams move from prototype to real-world deployment.

Core Tracks
<br></br>
- DeFi Track

Prize Pool: 10,000 USDT + Official Post-Hackathon Incubation Support
<br></br>
Decentralized finance is reshaping the foundational logic of global value transfer through its core advantages of transparency, efficiency, and permissionless access. This track focuses on RWA-oriented applications built on HashKey Chain, including but not limited to stocks, real estate, precious metals, money market funds, and stablecoin applications. We sincerely invite developers, product designers, and financial architecture specialists deeply engaged in the DeFi space to join.
<br></br>
- PayFi Track

Prize Pool: 10,000 USDT + Official Post-Hackathon Incubation Support
<br></br>
The PayFi track leverages HSP (HashKey Settlement Protocol) as its technical foundation to create a one-stop digital asset payment and settlement solution, unifying payment requests, confirmations, receipts, and other processes in Web3 scenarios. HSP does not manage funds — it is solely responsible for the transmission, verification, and status synchronization of payment interaction messages. For the HSP user manual, visit the top navigation bar at https://hashfans.io/.
<br></br>
- AI Track

Prize Pool: 10,000 USDT + Official Post-Hackathon Incubation Support
<br></br>
We are seeking innovations including AI-based automatic payment systems, AI-powered intelligent trading strategies, and AI-automated operation and maintenance for DeFi protocols. We invite participants to bring cutting-edge AI concepts together with real-world application scenarios to build disruptive, high-potential projects, collaborating with like-minded peers to unlock the full value of AI across broader domains.
<br></br>
- ZKID Track

Prize Pool: 10,000 USDT + Official Post-Hackathon Incubation Support
<br></br>
Zero-Knowledge Proof serves as the core underlying technology in the Web3 privacy track. With its core advantage of "proving without leaking," it is reshaping the underlying paradigm of blockchain privacy protection and trusted computing, resolving the fundamental contradiction between transparency and privacy. We sincerely invite algorithm engineers, protocol architects, and zero-knowledge proof researchers focused on ZK cryptography, privacy computing, and blockchain development to join this track. Participants are encouraged to build with NexaID: https://nexaid.gitbook.io/nexaid-docs/
<br></br>

四大核心賽道
<br></br>
- DeFi 賽道

獎金池：10,000 USDT + 後續官方孵化支持
<br></br>
去中心化金融（DeFi）正以透明、高效、無門檻的核心優勢，重塑全球價值流轉的底層邏輯。本賽道聚焦基於 HashKey Chain 的 RWA 導向應用，涵蓋但不限於股票、房地產、貴金屬、貨幣市場基金及穩定幣應用。我們誠摯邀請深耕 DeFi 領域的開發者、產品設計師與金融邏輯架構師加入。

- 支付賽道

獎金池：10,000 USDT + 後續官方孵化支持
<br></br>
支付賽道以 HSP（HashKey Settlement Protocol）為技術底座，構建一站式數字資產支付與資金結算方案，統一 Web3 場景下的支付請求、確認、收款等交互流程。HSP 不托管資金，僅負責支付交互消息的傳遞、驗證與狀態同步。HSP 用戶手冊請訪問 https://hashfans.io/ 頂部導航欄查閱。

- AI 賽道

獎金池：10,000 USDT + 後續官方孵化支持
<br></br>
我們在尋找 AI 自動支付、AI 驅動的智能交易策略、DeFi 協議的 AI 自動化運維等方向的創新項目。我們期待參賽者帶著前沿的 AI 技術理念，結合實際應用場景，打造出具備顛覆性潛力的創新成果，在這裏與同頻夥伴碰撞思想、協同攻堅，讓 AI 技術的價值在更多領域充分釋放。

- ZKID 賽道

獎金池：10,000 USDT + 後續官方孵化支持
<br></br>
零知識證明（ZK）作為 Web3 隱私賽道的核心底層技術，以"證明而不泄露"的核心優勢，重構區塊鏈隱私保護與可信計算的底層範式，破解透明性與隱私性的核心矛盾。我們誠摯邀請深耕 ZK 密碼學、隱私計算、區塊鏈底層開發的算法工程師、協議架構師與零知識證明研發者加入賽道，聚焦 ZK 驅動的鏈上隱私交易協議、隱私智能合約等方向的開發。參賽者可使用 NexaID 進行構建：https://nexaid.gitbook.io/nexaid-docs/
<br></br>
