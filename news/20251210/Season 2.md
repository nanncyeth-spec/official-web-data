## [Announcement] $HSK Staking Program Season 2 Officially Launches, Unlocking New On-Chain Ecosystem Rewards

December 10, 2025 — HashKey Chain has officially launched the $HSK staking program season 2. The upgrade delivers comprehensive improvements to the yield structure, allocation transparency, and incentive mechanisms. It also introduces an ecosystem incentive pool and loyalty rewards, offering participants a more stable, fair, and sustainable staking experience.

Comprehensive Enhancement of the Reward Structure:
The upgraded $HSK staking program adopts a fixed-rate model. Unlike previous floating-rate structures affected by market fluctuations, the current APY is stable, transparent, and predictable, allowing participants to earn consistent rewards under clear, well-defined rules.
There are no entry barriers, and users can start staking with just 1 $HSK and begin earning immediately. The expected annualized yield is 5%–8%, with a fixed APY of 5% and an additional 1%-3% APY allocated as long-term user incentive rewards.
Long-Term User Incentives and Reward Mechanisms
To further reward users who have continued to support the ecosystem, this round of the staking program introduces a Loyalty Bonus. Participants from the previous staking cycle will receive a one-time additional reward at the end of the current cycle, calculated based on their staking duration in the previous round.
The bonus tiers are as follows:
- Previous staking term of 365 days: +3% one-time bonus
- Previous staking term of 180 days: +2% one-time bonus
- Previous staking term of 30 or 90 days: +1% one-time bonus
Note: All bonus rates are determined by the user’s actual staking record from the previous cycle. The bonus will be distributed in a single payment upon completion of the current staking period.

In addition, this round introduces an ecosystem-specific incentive pool funded by the portion of rewards deducted when users unlock early. Under the new rules, if a user unstakes before the end of the staking period, 50% of any earned rewards will be deducted. If rewards have already been claimed, the corresponding penalty will be deducted from the principal. All deducted amounts will be allocated to the ecosystem incentive pool and distributed proportionally to participants who complete the full staking period.
This mechanism encourages long-term participation while enhancing the sustainability of the overall incentive cycle.

Staking Period and Reward Distribution Rules:
1. Staking Period: 365 days. 
2. Reward Calculation: Rewards accrue linearly on a daily basis and become available for withdrawal on a T+1 schedule.
3. Claiming Method: Users can claim the unlocked rewards at any time following the T+1 rule.

The flexible, transparent, and traceable reward model ensures that all participants earn verifiable and sustainable on-chain returns.

How to Participate
4. Staking Window: Dec 11, 12:00 UTC — Dec 18, 12:00 UTC
5. Staking Channel: https://hashkeychain.net/hskstaking 

The upgraded staking model is open for only 7 days in this round, and the total capacity of the staking pool is limited. Once the cap is reached, no additional staking will be accepted. Users need to complete their staking during the open window to secure the full upgraded yields and related incentives for this cycle.

## $HSK质押计划第二期正式升级上线，全链生态回馈开启
<br></br>
2025年12月10日，HashKey Chain正式推出$HSK质押计划第二期。此次升级从收益结构、分配透明度到生态激励机制都进行了系统性的优化，并新增生态激励池和忠诚用户奖励，为所有参与者提供更稳定、更公平、更具有持续激增长的质押体验。

收益结构全面升级：固定利率模型，更稳定、更可预期
本期 $HSK 质押计划采用固定利率的模式，相较过去可能受到市场波动影响的浮动模型，本轮 APY 更加稳定、透明、可预期，确保所有质押者都能在明晰的规则下获得稳定收益。
本期质押无门槛限制，用户仅需持有至少 1 枚 $HSK 即可参与并开始获取收益。本轮预计年化收益率为 5%–8%，其中基础 APY 为 5%，其余部分为长期用户激励奖励。

长期用户激励：忠诚奖励与生态激励池双重加成
为了进一步回馈长期支持生态的用户，本轮质押计划增设了忠实用户奖励机制。曾参与上一轮质押的用户，均可在本轮质押期满后按其在上一轮的质押时长获得一次性额外奖励，具体规则如下：
  - 上一轮质押期限为 365 天：额外一次性奖励 +3%
  - 上一轮质押期限为 180 天：额外一次性奖励 +2%
  - 上一轮质押期限为 30 天 或 90 天：额外一次性奖励 +1%
说明：奖励以用户在上一轮的实际质押记录为准，奖励将在本轮质押到期后一次性发放。

此外，本轮质押还引入了生态专项激励池，其来源为提前解锁时产生的收益扣减部分。根据新规则，若用户在质押周期结束前提前解锁，已获得收益将扣减 50%；如用户已提前领取收益，则相应罚没将从本金中扣除。被扣除的部分将全部注入生态专项激励池，并在本轮质押结束后，按比例分配给全程完成质押的参与者。
这一机制既强化了对长期参与者的激励，也确保生态内部激励循环的可持续性。

质押周期与收益发放规则：
1. 质押周期：365 天
2. 收益计算：收益线性解锁并每日计算，收益按日产生，即T+1可提取。
3. 领取方式：用户可按照按 T+1 规则，随时领取已解锁的收益。
灵活、透明、可追踪的收益结构，确保所有质押用户都能在链上获得可验证、可持续的回报。

如何参与
1. 质押开启时间：2025年12月11日 12:00 UTC —2025年12月18日  12:00 UTC 
2. 质押通道：https://hashkeychain.net/hskstaking 

本期升级后的质押模式仅开放7天，且质押池的总额度有限，达到上限后将不再接受新的参与者。用户需在开放期内完成质押，以确保获得本轮升级后的完整收益与相关激励。
