Bringing Real-World Web2 Data On-Chain with zkTLS
<br></br>

With its deployment on HashKey Chain, the Primus dApp now enables:

- Generation of verifiable on-chain proofs from Web2 data sources
- Ensuring data integrity and authenticity without exposing private information
- Secure integration of real-world data into the HashKey Chain ecosystem for developers and institutions

This capability establishes a critical technical foundation for on-chain financial applications requiring high compliance, strong privacy, and reliable data.
